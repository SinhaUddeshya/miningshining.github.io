Services:


Mining and Geology

1) Geological Exploration Services to support the process related to the identification and development of mineral resources.
2) Geological Mapping of the area.
3) Resource and Reserve Assessment of Minerals based on Mining Rules.
4) Due diligence of the Mineral Wealth and Mineral related Properties.
5) General Survey of Mines, Plants, Road , Other Projects.
6) Preparation of Mine Plan.
7) Preparation of Mine Closure Plan (Progressive and Final).
8) Land Use Planning.
9) Mine and Industrial Environmental Closure Cost Assessments.
10) Legal assistance in course of Mining Planning and Mining.
11) Development and Maintenance of Integrated Environmental Management Programs.
12) Effective Monitoring of the Plans.
13) Liaison with concerned Govt. / non - Govt. officials to help the Clients for getting the Concessions.

Geohydrology

1) Groundwater feasibility involving the Study and Assessment of the Availability, Quality and Quantity of groundwater.
2) Study of geological controls on its occurrence and movements.
3) Management of Ground Water.

Environment

1) Preparation of Environmental proposals for filing at various levels to get the Environmental Clearance.
2) Presentation at various Authority for getting the approval.

Forest

Preparation of Forest Diversion Proposal and its presentation at various levels till approval.
